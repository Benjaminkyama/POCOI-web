About Us

Positive Community Impact Organisation (POCOI) is a Community Based Organization (CBO) in Makueni County, working to break the cycle of poverty through sustainable programs. We believe that every individual deserves access to essential resources such as economic opportunities. Through innovative, community driven programs, we aim to create lasting positive change and ensure that poverty is no longer a barrier to a fulfilling life.
